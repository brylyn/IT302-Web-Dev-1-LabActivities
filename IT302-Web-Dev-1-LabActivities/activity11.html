THE BIBLE

The Bible contains many inspirational and challenging shorter verses that are great for memorization or sharing on social media. Even though these passages from the Bible are short, they can still work in amazing ways to transform the lives of those seeking Jesus! Try to memorize these short Bible verses and discover God's strength and peace throughout your day as you recall His promises!

Jeremiah 29:11

For I know the plans I have for you,” declares the LORD, “plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future.
